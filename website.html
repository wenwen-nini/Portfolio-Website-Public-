Protecting LIfe Below Water (SDG #14)

Oceans: The Heart of Our Planet

Oceans cover over 70% of our planet and are essential to life as we know it. They regulate the Earth's climate, provide food and livelihoods for billions, and host an incredible diversity of species. However, marine ecosystems face threats like pollution, overfishing, and climate change.

Did you know?
An estimation of 8-10 million metric tons of plastic ends up in the ocean each year.

More than 90% of the world's fish stocks are either fully exploited or overfished.

Coral reefs could vanish by 2050 if global warming continues at its current pace.
